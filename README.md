# 💖 HeartFeel (a quiet space)

**HeartFeel** is not a chatbot.
It’s a quiet space to feel, write, and reflect.

A simple, human-centered web app where you can express what’s inside you and receive a gentle reflection back — without pressure, noise, or complexity.

---

## 🌙 About

Sometimes, you don’t need answers.
You just need a place where your feelings can exist.

HeartFeel is built for that moment.

* No login
* No data storage
* No judgment
* Just you and your thoughts

---

## ✨ Features

### 💬 Express Freely

Write what’s on your mind — raw, unfiltered, real.

### 🎭 Emotion-Based Reflection

Pick how you feel:

* 💔 Sad
* 😊 Happy
* 😔 Lonely
* ❤️ Love
* 😤 Confused
* 🌙 Calm

And get a soft, human-like reflection.

### 💭 Gentle Responses

Instead of “answers”, HeartFeel gives:

* emotionally aware lines
* supportive reflections
* simple, calming words

### 🔒 Private by Design

* Nothing is stored
* No backend
* Runs fully in your browser

### 📋 Copy & Share

* Copy your reflection 💌
* Share quietly with someone

---

## 🧠 How It Works

HeartFeel uses a **rule-based reflection system** (not LLM-based).

* Emotion selection → guides tone
* User input → adds context
* Pre-written emotional patterns → generate response
* Light randomness → avoids repetition

It also includes:

* local memory (to reduce repeated lines)
* soft variations for natural feel
* simple keyword-based emotional enhancement
